 Disasters and accidents 
 
 2024 Dhaka building fire 
 At least 43 people are killed in a building fire in Dhaka , Bangladesh . (BBC News) 
 A Sikorsky S-92 helicopter crashes six kilometers off the coast of Bergen , Norway , killing one and injuring five others. (NU.nl)
